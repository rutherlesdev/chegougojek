How It Works

  • Register on the App / Login Register on the app using your basic contact details which include your name, e-mail address and phone number or login by simply using your social accounts like Facebook, Twitter or Gmail
  • Enter the Application and Place Your Order Once you have logged into the application, type in your requirements and place your order mentioning your address details along with the nearest landmark
  • Track Your Order After successfully placing your order, track and find out the expected time of arrival along with the location where your order has reached
